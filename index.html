Who we are

ABOUT US

DLOK is a social commerce platform dedicated to enhancing the quality of life in rural/semi-urban households. Our main objective is to bring modern amenities and sustainable solutions to remote villages/semi-urban areas, ensuring equitable access to high-quality, climate-friendly, and gender-inclusive products and services.

Central to our approach is the empowerment of rural entrepreneurs, whom we invest in as vital connectors to their communities. These entrepreneurs serve as the bridge between our platform and rural households, facilitating access to a range of essential offerings, including clean energy services and other life-improving products.

Our Goal

Our goal is to achieve 3 million happy customers using solar energy

Our Values

Our Mission

Our Benefits

Solar energy is a key driver of sustainable development. It provides a clean, renewable source of power, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and lowering greenhouse gas emissions.
Additionally, solar energy promotes energy independence and can create jobs, boosting local economies.
